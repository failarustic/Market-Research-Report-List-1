<p><ul><li>Rotary Type</li><li>Foil Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry electric shaver market is primarily divided into two types: rotary and foil shavers. Rotary shavers feature circular cutting heads that adapt to the contours of the face, making them ideal for longer or denser hair. They provide a close shave and are typically suited for wet or dry use. Foil shavers utilize oscillating blades beneath a thin metallic foil, ensuring a smooth shave for shorter hair. They are often favored for their precision and speed, catering to various grooming preferences.</p></p>
