<p><strong>The Waterjet Cutting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressurized Water</li><li>Abrasive Mixture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting machinery market comprises two primary types: pressurized water cutting and abrasive mixture cutting. Pressurized water cutting utilizes high-pressure water streams to cut soft materials like rubber and foam, achieving precise, clean cuts without heat-affected zones. In contrast, abrasive mixture cutting incorporates abrasive particles in the water stream, enabling it to cut through tougher materials such as metal and stone. Both methods offer versatility and precision, catering to various industrial applications and enhancing manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
