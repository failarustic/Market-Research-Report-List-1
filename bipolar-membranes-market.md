<p><p>The bipolar membranes market is primarily divided into two types: homogeneous and heterogeneous bipolar membranes. Homogeneous bipolar membranes consist of a uniform polymeric structure, providing consistent ion separation and enhancing performance in applications like electrodialysis. In contrast, heterogeneous bipolar membranes feature distinct layers, often combining materials with different properties to optimize ion transport and selectivity. Both types serve various industries, including water treatment, food processing, and energy generation, offering unique advantages tailored to specific separation and purification processes.</p></p>
