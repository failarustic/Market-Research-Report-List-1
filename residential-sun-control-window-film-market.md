<p><p>The residential sun control window film market comprises three main types: Clear (Non-Reflective), Dyed (Non-Reflective), and Vacuum Coated (Reflective). Clear films provide UV protection without altering appearance, ideal for maintaining natural light. Dyed films absorb solar heat and reduce glare while offering privacy, suitable for residential settings. Vacuum Coated films feature a reflective surface that effectively blocks heat and glare, enhancing energy efficiency. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing aesthetics, comfort, and energy savings in homes.</p></p>
