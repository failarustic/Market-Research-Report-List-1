<p><strong>The UV-NIL Photoresist Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist</li><li>Negative Photoresist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-NIL (Ultra-Violet Nanoimprint Lithography) photoresist market is primarily divided into two types: positive and negative photoresists. Positive photoresists become soluble in developer solutions when exposed to UV light, allowing for precise pattern formation during imprinting. Conversely, negative photoresists polymerize upon UV exposure, rendering them insoluble and enabling the creation of patterns that correspond to the exposed areas. Both types are essential in advanced semiconductor manufacturing and nanofabrication, catering to various applications requiring high-resolution patterns.</p></p>
