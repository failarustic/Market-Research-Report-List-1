<p><strong>The Breast Cancer Screening Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Screen Mammography</li><li>Digital Mammography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breast cancer screening equipment primarily includes Film Screen Mammography and Digital Mammography. Film Screen Mammography uses traditional x-ray film to capture images of breast tissue, offering a cost-effective approach. However, it has limitations in image clarity and requires careful handling of films. In contrast, Digital Mammography captures images electronically, providing superior image quality and allowing radiologists to manipulate the images for better diagnosis. This technology enhances detection rates, making it increasingly preferred in modern breast cancer screening practices.</p></p>
