<p><p>Plunger sensors are critical in various industrial applications, categorized primarily into 2-wire and 3-wire sensors, as well as rattle sensors. 2-wire sensors are simple in structure and require minimal wiring, making them cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, 3-wire sensors offer enhanced functionality and precise measurements, suited for more complex setups. Rattle sensors, which detect vibrations or movement, provide additional monitoring capabilities in dynamic environments.
