Pipe Network Systems use a network of pipes to distribute heptafluoropropane gas throughout the facility, ensuring comprehensive coverage in case of a fire. In contrast, Cabinet Independent Systems are self-contained units installed near sensitive equipment, offering targeted protection. Both systems effectively suppress fires while minimizing damage to electronic equipment, making them vital for data center safety.</p></p>
