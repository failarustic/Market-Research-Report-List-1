<p><p>The nanofiber market encompasses various technologies for fiber production. Electrospinning uses electric fields to create fine fibers from polymer solutions. Spinning biocomponent fibers combines different materials in one process. Melt spinning extrudes molten polymers to form fibers. Template synthesis molds fibers using a pre-existing structure. Electroblowing enhances electrospinning with air jets to improve fiber morphology. Self-assembly relies on molecular interactions to organize materials into fibers. Forcespinning utilizes centrifugal forces for faster production. Other methods include emerging techniques tailored for specific applications.</p></p>
