<p><ul><li>"Regular Lyocell Fiber"</li><li>"Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly lyocell fiber market comprises two main types: regular lyocell fiber and crosslinked lyocell fiber. Regular lyocell fiber is produced through a solvent-spinning process using environmentally friendly solvents, making it biodegradable and sustainably sourced. Crosslinked lyocell fiber, on the other hand, is chemically modified to enhance its properties, such as durability and moisture management, while retaining eco-friendliness. Both types serve various applications in textiles and apparel, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers seeking sustainable alternatives.</p></p>
