<p><strong>The Radiation Dose Monitoring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dosimeters</li><li>Area Process Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation dose monitoring involves two key market types: dosimeters and area process monitors. Dosimeters are small, portable devices used by individuals to measure exposure to radiation over time, ensuring safety in various environments. Area process monitors, on the other hand, are fixed systems installed in specific locations to continuously assess and display radiation levels in the surrounding area, providing real-time data for safety management. Together, they play a crucial role in occupational health, environmental monitoring, and emergency response scenarios.</p></p>
