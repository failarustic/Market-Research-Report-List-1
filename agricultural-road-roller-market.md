<p><p>The agricultural road roller market comprises two main types: hydraulic rollers and non-hydraulic rollers. Hydraulic rollers utilize hydraulic systems to generate force, allowing for efficient compaction of soil and agricultural surfaces, making them suitable for heavy-duty tasks. Non-hydraulic rollers, on the other hand, rely on mechanical systems and are generally lighter, making them ideal for smaller projects or maintenance tasks. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing soil stability, crop productivity, and farm infrastructure development.</p></p>
