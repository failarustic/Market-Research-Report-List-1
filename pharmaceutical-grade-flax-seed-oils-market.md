<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade flaxseed oils market primarily employs two extraction methods: the squeezing method and the leaching method. The squeezing method, also known as cold pressing, involves mechanically pressing the seeds to extract oil while retaining nutrients. In contrast, the leaching method utilizes solvents to dissolve the oil from the seeds, often resulting in higher yields but potentially affecting oil purity. Both methods cater to different market needs, emphasizing quality, potency, and application in dietary supplements and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
