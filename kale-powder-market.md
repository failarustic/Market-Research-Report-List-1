<p><p>The kale powder market can be segmented into two primary types: air-dried and freeze-dried. Air-dried kale powder involves removing moisture at low temperatures, preserving flavor and nutrients, though some may be lost during the process. In contrast, freeze-dried kale powder retains more nutrients and flavor by quickly freezing the leaves and then sublimating the ice. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking convenient and nutritious options, with varying shelf life, taste, and nutrient retention profiles.</p></p>
