<p><p>The infrared thermometer market is segmented into ear, forehead, and multifunction types. Ear thermometers measure temperature via the ear canal, offering quick and accurate readings, making them suitable for infants and children. Forehead thermometers utilize non-contact technology to gauge surface temperature, ideal for quick screening in various settings. Multifunction thermometers combine features of both ear and forehead options, often including additional capabilities like room and object temperature measurement, providing versatility for both home and clinical use.</p></p>
