<p><p>The Logistics Automatic Sorting System market includes various mechanisms to efficiently sort items in warehouses and distribution centers. The Linear Sorting System employs a straight-line conveyor layout, ideal for high-throughput operations with minimal space. In contrast, the Cross Belt Sorting System utilizes a grid of belts that can divert items to multiple paths, offering flexibility and speed for diverse product sizes. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve order accuracy in supply chain management.</p></p>
