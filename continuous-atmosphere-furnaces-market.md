<p><p>Continuous atmosphere furnaces are essential in industrial thermal processing, offering different designs for varying applications. Belt furnaces utilize a moving belt to convey materials through heating zones, ideal for continuous production. Rotating hearth furnaces feature a turning platform, maximizing heat transfer for uniform heating of bulky items. Pusher furnaces employ hydraulic systems to push loaded trays through heated chambers, suitable for high-volume operations. Other types include batch furnaces and specialized designs tailored for specific materials and processes.</p></p>
