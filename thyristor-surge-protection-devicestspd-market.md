<p><p>Thyristor Surge Protection Devices (TSPD) are specialized protection devices designed to safeguard electrical equipment from voltage surges or transients. The TSPD market is divided into two primary types: bidirectional TSPDs and unidirectional TSPDs. Bidirectional TSPDs can handle surges in both directions, making them suitable for applications with AC or fluctuating DC currents. In contrast, unidirectional TSPDs are designed for specific applications with one-way current flow, providing effective protection primarily in DC systems or applications where reverse surges are not a concern.</p></p>
