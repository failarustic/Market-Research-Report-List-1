<p><p>The rugs and carpets market is categorized into four main types: woven, needle felt, tufted, and others. Woven carpets are crafted using traditional techniques on looms, offering durability and intricate designs. Needle felt carpets are made from synthetic fibers bonded together, ideal for commercial use due to their resilience. Tufted carpets involve a process where yarn is stitched into a backing, providing a wide range of styles and textures. The "others" category includes specialty carpets like shag and outdoor varieties.</p></p>
