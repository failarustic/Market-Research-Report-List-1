<p><p>The epoxy hardener market comprises three primary types: amine-based products, anhydride-based products, and other formulations. Amine-based products, known for their excellent adhesion and chemical resistance, are commonly used in coatings and adhesives. Anhydride-based products offer high thermal stability and are often employed in industrial applications. Other types include polymercaptans and phenolic hardeners, which provide specialized properties for niche applications. Together, these products enhance the performance of epoxy resins across various industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
