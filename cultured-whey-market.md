<p><strong>The Cultured Whey Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Isolate</li><li>Whey Hydrolysate</li><li>Whey Concentrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cultured whey market comprises three main types: Whey Isolate, Whey Hydrolysate, and Whey Concentrate. Whey Isolate is processed to remove most fats and lactose, offering a high protein content. Whey Hydrolysate undergoes partial hydrolysis, making it easier to digest and often used in sports nutrition. Whey Concentrate contains lower protein levels and retains some fat and lactose, making it a cost-effective source of protein. Each type serves different consumer needs, from athletes to those seeking general health benefits.</p></p>
