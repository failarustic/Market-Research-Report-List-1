Castle stone offers a more rustic, chiseled look with uneven shapes and sizes, perfect for traditional and historical aesthetics. Splitface veneer, with its rough, textured surface, enhances visual interest and adds depth, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. Together, these types cater to diverse architectural styles.</p></p>
