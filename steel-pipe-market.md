<p><p>The steel pipe market comprises primarily two types: welded steel pipes and seamless steel pipes. Welded steel pipes are created by rolling steel plates and welding the edges together, ideal for structural applications due to their cost-effectiveness. Seamless steel pipes, on the other hand, are manufactured without joints, providing greater strength and reliability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, influencing their demand in various sectors such as construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
