<p><p>The natural flavor extract market comprises two main types: dry flavor extracts and liquid flavor extracts. Dry flavor extracts are concentrated powders derived from natural sources, utilized in products like baked goods, snacks, and beverage powders, offering an intense flavor without added moisture. Liquid flavor extracts, on the other hand, are typically oils or syrups, used in a wider range of applications such as beverages, sauces, and desserts, providing versatility and ease of blending with other ingredients. Both types cater to the growing demand for natural and clean-label products.</p></p>
