<p><p>The UV protective tapes market consists of two main types: single-sided and double-sided tapes. Single-sided UV protective tapes feature adhesive on one side, making them ideal for applications where one surface needs protection against UV damage, such as window films and outdoor signage. Double-sided UV protective tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on both sides, allowing them to bond two surfaces while providing UV resistance, commonly used in mounting, laminating, and other industrial applications for enhanced durability.</p></p>
