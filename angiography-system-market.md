<p><p>The angiography system market encompasses various specialized types, each targeting specific anatomical regions. Head part angiography focuses on visualizing cerebral blood vessels to diagnose conditions like aneurysms and strokes. Chest angiography primarily examines the heart and pulmonary arteries, crucial for diagnosing cardiovascular diseases. Abdominal angiography evaluates blood flow in the abdominal region, aiding in the assessment of conditions like aneurysms and organ perfusion issues. Heart angiography, also known as coronary angiography, is essential for diagnosing coronary artery disease and guiding interventions.</p></p>
