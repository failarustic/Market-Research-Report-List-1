<p><p>IC packaging substrates are crucial for connecting integrated circuits to their substrates, providing electrical and thermal management. The market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Organic Substrate** - Typically made from polymers, these are lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for high-density applications.</p><p>2. **Inorganic Substrate** - Comprising materials like ceramics or glass, these offer superior thermal performance and stability, ideal for high-power applications.</p><p>3. **Composite Substrate** - Combining both organic and inorganic materials, these substrates deliver enhanced performance characteristics tailored for specific applications, balancing cost and functionality.</p></p>
