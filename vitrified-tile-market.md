<p><p>Vitrified tiles are a type of ceramic tile that undergo a process of vitrification, which involves heating the tiles at high temperatures to create a dense and non-porous surface. Due to their aesthetic appeal and functionality, vitrified tiles have gained significant popularity in residential and commercial sectors.</p><p>The Vitrified Tile Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.5% during the forecast period.
