<p><p>The Portable Ambulatory Ventilators market is classified into three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. </p><p>Electronic ventilators utilize advanced microprocessors for precise control and monitoring, making them ideal for various patient needs. Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed gas, providing a simpler design often preferred in emergency situations. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both electronic and pneumatic functions, offering flexibility and enhanced performance for patient care. Each type caters to different clinical scenarios, ensuring effective respiratory support in ambulatory settings.</p></p>
