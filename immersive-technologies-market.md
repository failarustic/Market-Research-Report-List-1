<p><p>The immersive technologies market encompasses three primary types: Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR). VR creates fully immersive digital environments, isolating users from the physical world. AR overlays digital information onto the real world, enhancing the user's perception without complete immersion. MR combines elements of both VR and AR, allowing real and virtual elements to interact in real-time, creating a more blended experience. Each market serves distinct applications across gaming, education, healthcare, and various industries.</p></p>
