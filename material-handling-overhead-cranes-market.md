<p><p>The material handling overhead cranes market comprises primarily two types: single-girder and double-girder overhead cranes. Single-girder cranes utilize a single beam for lifting, making them cost-effective and suitable for lighter loads and smaller facilities. In contrast, double-girder cranes feature two beams, offering higher load capacities and greater stability, ideal for heavy-duty applications in larger industrial settings. Both types enhance efficiency in material movement, optimizing operations across various industries while addressing specific lifting and space requirements.</p></p>
