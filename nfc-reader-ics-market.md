<p><strong>The NFC Reader ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full NFC</li><li>ISO/IEC 15693</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NFC reader IC market encompasses various types, including Full NFC, which supports a comprehensive range of NFC protocols, enabling versatile applications. ISO/IEC 15693 is designed for vicinity coupling at longer ranges, suitable for smart labels and RFID systems. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A focuses on contactless smart cards, ideal for access control and payment systems, while ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B supports both A and B communication protocols, enhancing interoperability in diverse applications, including public transport and secure transactions.</p></p>
