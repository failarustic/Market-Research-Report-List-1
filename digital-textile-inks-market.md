<p><p>The digital textile inks market encompasses various types tailored for specific fabric types and applications. Dispersed inks are ideal for synthetics like polyester, providing vibrant colors. Reactive inks bond chemically with fibers, making them suitable for natural fabrics, particularly cotton. Acid inks are designed for protein fibers such as silk and wool, ensuring rich shades and durability. Pigment inks offer versatility across multiple substrates with excellent lightfastness, making them popular for a wide range of textile applications.</p></p>
