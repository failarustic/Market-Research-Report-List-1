<p><p>The solid plastic bottle market encompasses various materials, each with distinct properties and applications. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) is widely used for beverages due to its lightweight and recyclability. PP (polypropylene) offers high chemical resistance, making it ideal for personal care products. HDPE (high-density polyethylene) is durable and suitable for containers like milk and detergents. LDPE (low-density polyethylene) is flexible, often used for squeeze bottles. Other materials may include polycarbonate and bioplastics, catering to specialized packaging needs across various industries.</p></p>
