<p><ul><li>Welded Stainless Steel Tube</li><li>Seamless Stainless Steel Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small diameter stainless steel tubing market consists of two primary types: welded and seamless stainless steel tubes. Welded stainless steel tubes are produced by joining stainless steel strips or sheets, resulting in efficient production and varied sizes. They are ideal for applications requiring lower pressure. Seamless stainless steel tubes, on the other hand, are manufactured without joints, offering superior strength and pressure resistance, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and medical.</p></p>
