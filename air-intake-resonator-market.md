<p><ul><li>In-Line Resonators</li><li>Side Branch Resonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air intake resonator market includes two primary types: in-line resonators and side branch resonators. In-line resonators are integrated directly into the air intake path, focusing on tuning the air flow and reducing noise while optimizing engine performance. Side branch resonators, on the other hand, are attached to the intake system at a perpendicular angle, targeting specific sound frequencies for noise reduction. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing overall engine efficiency and improving vehicle acoustics.</p></p>
