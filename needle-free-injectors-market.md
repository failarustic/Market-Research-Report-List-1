<p><p>Needle-free injectors are categorized into liquid-based and powder-based markets, each serving distinct applications. Liquid-based injectors deliver vaccines and medications in a pre-filled liquid form, offering quick and painless administration. They are ideal for administering biologics and need precise dosage. In contrast, powder-based injectors utilize a solid form of medication, which is converted to aerosol or fine mist for delivery, often used for vaccines and biologics requiring stability. Both types enhance patient comfort and compliance in medical treatments.</p></p>
